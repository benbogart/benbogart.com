The Tango Music Project

What is the Tango Music Project?

The tango music project is for everyone aspiring tango musicians everywhere. It involves everything from bandoneon and tango learning resources which are unavailable or difficult to find outside of Argentina and lead sheets and full scores for groups looking for material to articles on the process of learning to play tango and the bandoneon.

The specific projects planned right now are:

  • The Open Tango Real Book Project
    • A Tango Real Book (book of tango lead sheets) that anyone can contribute to. Coming August 2007
  • WikiTangoMusic
    • Want to learn more about tango music. Do you have info to share on playing tango, or on discographies or the history? Share and find verifiable tango music information with everyone. Coming July 2007
