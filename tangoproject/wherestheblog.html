The Tango Music Project

So Where's the Blog???

I've been hearing this question a lot since the Tango Music Project Began. Well, there is no blog, and neither Thuy nor I has any plans to start one. Why? We already too much going on. For anyone interested in what, this is what we are up to.

I am practicing 4-4.5 hours a day on the bandoneón, which seems to be the minimum to keep up with my once a week lesson with Rodolfo Mederos, and the maximum my body can currently handle. In addition I spend 1.5 hours a day transcribing tango scores preparing for a tour with Los Gatos Azules in October. I am teaching online saxophone lessons which is another 1-3 hours a day, and in the evening there is some time for Thuy and I to spend together or to go dancing.

For what Thuy is up to visit her Fluid Movement web site.
